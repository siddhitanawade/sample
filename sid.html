CAKE RECIPE

cakes are my all time favourite recipe,so this is a simple cake recipe

Ingredients:-

  1. flour ✔️
  2. oil ✔️
  3. sugar ✔️
  4. baking powder ✔️
  5. baking soda ✔️
  6. milk ✔️

Recipe:-

  • add flour
  • add sugar in it
  • add baking powder
  • add baking soda
  • add oil
  • mix all ingredients
  • add milk
  • mix well
  • pour the batter in vessel
  • bake it for 20mins in oven
    • black forest cake
    • red velvet cake
